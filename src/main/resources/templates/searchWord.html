  • Searches are not case-sensitive. This means a search will include all occurrences of your word, whether capitalized or not.
  • An asterisk(*) is an indication of the omission of letters or words.
  • For example, if you search for 'ab*', it will give you all the words starting with 'ab-'.
  • On the other hand, you can search for '*tion', then you will get all the words ending with '-tion'.
  • For a more advanced search, you can try 'pre*tion' or '*form*', then the former one gives you words starting with 'pre-' AND ends with 'tion', and the latter one will return words containing 'form' in the middle.
